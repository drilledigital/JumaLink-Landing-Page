How JumaLink Works

JumaLink makes it easy for anyone to find reliable help and for artisans to access real opportunities. Whether you're looking for a trusted plumber, mason, carpenter, or mechanic, our platform connects you with skilled workers who are ready to deliver.

Step 1: Sign Up

Artisans create a profile showcasing their skills, experience, and availability. Clients post the work they need done, from home repairs to big projects. Signing up is quick, easy, and free.

Step 2: Get Matched

Artisans get notified about available jobs that match their skills, while clients can browse through verified profiles. Ratings, reviews, and past work help everyone choose the right fit.

Step 3: Connect & Work

Clients connect directly with artisans to discuss details and get the job done. Once completed, both sides can leave reviews, helping artisans grow their reputation and clients find reliable help every time.
